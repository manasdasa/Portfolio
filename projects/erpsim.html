The Challenge

In the IU ERPsim Tournament of Champions, competing teams ran a simulated consumer goods company in SAP, making real-time decisions about inventory, pricing, logistics, and sustainability KPIs — under time pressure with live competition.

My role as Stock Manager was to own all inventory and supply chain decisions: reorder levels, safety stock, vendor selection, and shipment scheduling — while monitoring financial performance.

Approach & Execution

📊
Real-Time Tableau Dashboards
Built live dashboards monitoring stock levels, shipment schedules, sustainability KPIs, and financial benchmarks. Updated every simulation round.
💹
Financial Modeling
Used Excel-based financial models to benchmark against competitors and optimize decisions for maximum company valuation — improved to $2M.
🎯
EY Consultant Presentation
Delivered executive-ready recommendations to EY Technology Consultants under timed conditions, defending our strategy and metrics.
